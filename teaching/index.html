/ / / / / Teaching

I have been tutoring and running courses on architectural and urban design in different Universities. Some were run as a traditional design studio where student have to respond to a design brief.

In the past few years I have been involved in creating a serie of workshops and course titled Street Life Studies. Their focus is on the use of public street spaces in cities around the world. Students observe place; as people, behaviours, systems, and spaces in both their formal and informal expressions.

/ / / / / Capacity building

Capacity building for construction workers is often a component of the project I have been involved in. I have organized and run courses on different topics related to construction and disaster preparedness. The key challenge in this type of activities is to find ways to communicate practical and theoretical knowledge to a wide and diverse audience. It is also essential to commmunicate the reasons behind guidelines and regulations so that workers do not only adopt them but understand their rationale.
